=== Comparison between SLR(1) and LR(1) ===

Number of SLR(1) states: 9
Number of LR(1) states: 9

SLR(1) reduces using FOLLOW sets (less precise)
LR(1) reduces using specific lookaheads (more precise)

LR(1) can handle grammars with shift/reduce or reduce/reduce conflicts
that SLR(1) cannot handle.
